Food Processing Machine by A. S. Engineering Works, Mumbai

Welcome to A. S. Engineering Works, Manufacturer and Exporter of Food Processing Machines. The company was established in 1992 at Mumbai. Sophisticated infrastructure facility and Ethical business practices make us unique in the industry. Haldiran Snacks Private Limited and Oberoi Flight Service are some of our prestigious clients and we are backed by Industry leading prices and prompt delivery. We offer a huge gamut of Namkeen, Potato Chips Making and Bakery Machines. Our Noodle Making, Papaya Peeling and Cubbing Machines are superior in performance. We also provide Banana Chips Machines, Hotel Restaurant and Kitchen Equipments that are highly efficient. Laundry Machines and Bhatti Gas Furnaces are acknowledged for their functionality. Our Sweets Making Machines and Namkeen Making Machine for Canteens are well known for their durability.
