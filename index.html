Established in the year 2018 at Mumbai, Maharashtra, we “A.S.ENGINEERING” are a Proprietorship based firm, engaged as the foremost Manufacturer of Flour Kneading Machine, Shawarma Machine, etc. Our products are high in demand due to their premium quality, seamless finish and affordable prices. Furthermore, we ensure to timely deliver these products to our clients, through this we have gained a huge clients base in the market.

Food Processing Machine

Our firm has been widely appreciated for manufacturing, supplying and exporting to the customers a wide range of best quality Food Processing Machines and Snacks Making Machines. In our offered range we have a wide variety of such machines like Namkeen Machinery, Potato Chips Plant, Bakery Machines, Papaiya Truity Fruity Plant, Hotel, Restaurant & Canteen Machinery.
